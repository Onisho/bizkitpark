Who we are

BIZKIT PARK pays tribute to one of the most illustrious sub genres ever to arise in modern alternative music: NU-METAL

Every classic track that helped shape this mind-blowing era receives the royal treatment in a swirling and seamless set. Limp Bizkit, Korn, System of a Down, Linkin Park, Slipknot, Papa Roach, Deftones, Disturbed, … None are left out of the party of Bizkit Park.

Despite being underestimated and often bashed by critics, Nu-Metal managed to provide the soundtrack for an entire generation. In addition, it turned out to be the last stronghold of heavy guitars in mainstream music. Gut punching riffs, aggressive drums and lots of attitude broadcasted by radio and TV on a daily basis… It’s a distant memory and hard to imagine these days.

Therefore one might conclude that it’s about time Nu-Metal received a proper salute. Henceforth Bizkit Park will take this task to heart with staggering enthusiasm and destroy every available stage in the process.

Bizkit Park is:
Niko Van Driessche
Bart Vriesacker
Jan De Sorte
Frederik Verhoeven
Servaas Steurbaut
